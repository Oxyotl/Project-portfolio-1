About us

We are a group of Scouts that love to explore and learn. Just come meet us and surely you will enjoy your stay!

  • Everyone’s welcome here. All genders, backgrounds and religions
  • Regardless of the child’s physical ability we are ready to show them what a Scout adventure is
  • Great importance is given to:
    • corporeity,creativity,spirituaity,personality
  • We give them the skills they need for schools, college, university, the job interview and in a broader sense: the skills they need for life
